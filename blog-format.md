> "Get up and eat some more, or the journey ahead will be too much for you." 1 Kings 19:7

Elijah had just done more for Israel than anyone in generations, but at the end of the day he hit his limits and he wanted to lay down and die.


> "Silence is the language of God, all else is poor translation." — Rumi

Today, I invite you to find five minutes of intentional silence. Not a silence filled with plans or worries, but a silence that simply *is*.
